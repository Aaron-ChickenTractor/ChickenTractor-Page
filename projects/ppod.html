PPOD

P2P file sharing without the overhead

Get Started

Overview

PPOD is a peer to peer file sharing tool that eliminates a lot of the overhead of alternatives like Google Drive. There is no login, no signup, and no server storing your files.

Key Features

🔒

No Login Required

Jump right in without creating accounts or providing any personal information.

🚫

No Cloud Storage

Your files never touch our servers - they transfer directly between peers.

🛡️

Privacy First

Choose who you share with by pairing devices via QR code. Only pair with devices you trust.

🎯

Minimal Overhead

Streamlined experience focused on getting your files where they need to go.

Technical Details

WebRTC

Direct peer-to-peer file transfers in real-time.

Handshake Server

Lightweight server facilitates device pairing.

Local or Browser Storage

Save files directly to your device or stash them in your browser for later download.

No Transfer Limit

Send files of any size, limited only by your device's storage.
