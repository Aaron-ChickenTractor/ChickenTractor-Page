Game of Life - Universes: Explore cellular automata with infinite possibilities

Explore Universes

Overview

GOLU stands for Game of Life - Universes, representing the concept that cellular automata can exist in different "universes" with varying physics rules. While Conway's original Game of Life uses the famous B3/S23 rule, this interactive explorer extends his work by allowing you to experiment with configurable neighborhood definitions and influence patterns.
